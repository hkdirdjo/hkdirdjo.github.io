Who Am I?

My name is Husayn and I am a mechanical engineering student at The University of British Columbia graduating in December 2022. Apparently, I am one of "those" engineers who loves what they do so much they are constantly tinkering on projects on top of the 9 to 5 ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ I chose mechanical engineering in general and the mechatronics specialization in particular in order to pursue my interests in aerospace and control systems.

I love science fiction of all stripes, from The Expanse to Person of Interest to Blindsight to Battletech to the Grim Darkness of the 41st millenium. In what little spare time I have left these days I enjoy watercolour painting, cycling, video games, and cooking.
